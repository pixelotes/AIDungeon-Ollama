It is 1888, and the London fog is as thick as pea soup. My name is Alistair Finch, a consulting detective often burdened with the bizarre cases that baffle Scotland Yard. The latest is a true conundrum: Lord Harrington vanished from his study overnight, a room locked and bolted from the inside, with the windows sealed shut.
