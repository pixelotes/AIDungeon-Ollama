Elara Vance is a xeno-botanist specializing in extinct flora, stationed on the desolate, ice-covered moon of Xylos, a forgotten research outpost. The colony’s primary function is extracting rare bioluminescent minerals, but a mysterious signal has been detected – a distress call from a long-lost research team. You’ve been tasked with investigating, but the outpost is eerily silent, coated in a strange, metallic dust.|||Your boots crunch on the crystalline surface as you approach the main control room, activating the antiquated scanner. A single, flickering holographic image of a vibrant purple flower appears, and you notice a faint energy signature emanating from the central console.
